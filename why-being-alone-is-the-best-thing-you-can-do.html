Why being alone is the best thing you can do

Published: Fri 30 December 2016
By Hrishikesh

In Life.

I have friends, but I enjoy being alone, I feel comfortable, I can be me.

I can't live in uncomfortable situations. A small defect or mishap in anything bothers my mind to a huge degree for some reason. So it's best for me to keep myself away from the randomness of the outer universe, and just try living in my own universe that is truly mine and no one can take it away from me, over which I have full control, i have to have that control. I need to live and be happy about living else rather be dead.
