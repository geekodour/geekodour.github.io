In Life.

We humans are stupid, we're letting ourselves to be manipulated by this pathetic thing called life. We don't even know what it is really, but humans decided to call it "life" which otherwise in my mothertoungue (i am bad at spellings, so i don't know if i spelled that right) is called "Jibon".

So what is life?

Take this micro example, I am talking universe and macro here so this example which might be a huge one in our life can be considered a micro example. Example: The Education System, I don't know much about other education systems but Indian education system sucks and the society supporting the wrong sides of this system can suck my dick. I am grateful for whatever I've learned but I have serious issues with the system, which I will make some big change someday. So sing with me, "The Indian Education System Sucks". Maybe we can talk about the education system in depth in another blog post but we were talking about life, what is life. So, there is this system which people can follow or not follow. The second one is a safer and smarter bet definetly and I really think NO one should be deprived from education. The point I am trying to make is, you can be a part of a system or else you can't be, but everything does not have to be 1s and 0s like in computerscience. In the deeper level it might even be, but according to a video I saw today everything in the universe is made up of 12 quantum particles and 4 different patterns (Now I am not physics genius, I don't even remember the G equation, but ya that's what the video says), it says things are random and universe is made up of randomness.

So back to the boys example, the first boy deprived of education can still get some education in some way or the other even if he was deprived of it. It's not a complete ON or OFF. The guy working at some MNC still can be deeply related to some cause related to the uplifment of the poor. So a human can and actually living in two different worlds, worlds are just state of mind. His mind has to think differently at work and when he's thinking about the poor. If his mind stays elsewhere, then he's unconcious and can be knocked down.

So, that's living in different worlds "inside" our minds. What about our physical bodies? What's life? I think, life is a world just like that. You know, people persuade you to use their product, life fucking microsoft almost got me there when I thought microsoft was everything I'll ever needand it is still dominating the market and it's a good thing, but If I never discovered linux I would never have known the goods.

Let me say that again, It plays your mind, if the sun looks grey because you installed "life" it looks red to you.
