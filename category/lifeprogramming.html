Changes in Study Pattern

Published: Fri 24 March 2017
By Hrishikesh

In Life,Programming.

All the 20 years of my life, I have been carrying this mentality to study something you have to study everything about it, making separate time for it. Well guess what, TIME TO CHANGE THIS. This mentality las affected me in numerous ways, for example If I have to text someone, I con't do other things I only text, If I am learning Regular Expressions, I am learning that for a days without learning anything else. This is a very bad study pattern, if you have it leave it. I am leaving it. It does no good to your brain, it has no befits on memory, you can go deep over time.

I am starting today, I'l post how successful I am with my new study pattern soon.
